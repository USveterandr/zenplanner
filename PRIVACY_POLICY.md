# Zen Planner - Privacy Policy

## Last Updated: March 5, 2026

### 1. Introduction

Zen Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Zen Planner when you use our mobile application and website.

### 2. Information We Collect

**Personal Information:**
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Name (optional, for personalization)
- Task lists, goals, and planning data you create

**Automatically Collected Information:**
- Device information (type, operating system, browser)
- Usage data (features used, time spent)
- Crash reports and performance data

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Zen Planner service
- Personalize your experience
- Send you important updates and notifications
- Improve our app based on user feedback
- Protect against fraud and abuse

### 4. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- You can delete your account and associated data at any time

### 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Vercel** - Hosting and infrastructure
- **Cloudflare** - Security and CDN

### 6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
