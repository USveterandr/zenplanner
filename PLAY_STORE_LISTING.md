Zen Planner - Your AI-Powered Productivity Companion

Beat procrastination and get more done with Zen Planner, the all-in-one task management app with built-in AI assistant.

KEY FEATURES:

✓ Smart Task Management
- Create tasks with priorities, due dates, and reminders
- Organize with categories and subtasks
- Drag and drop reordering

✓ AI Advisor
- Get personalized productivity tips
- Ask questions about time management
- Receive motivation and guidance

✓ Calendar View
- See your tasks and goals on a visual calendar
- Quick-add tasks directly from dates
- Monthly and weekly views

✓ Goal Tracking
- Set big-picture goals with milestones
- Track progress visually
- Celebrate achievements

✓ Habit Builder
- Build positive habits with daily tracking
- Streak counters to keep you motivated
- Custom frequencies (daily, weekly, etc.)

✓ Analytics Dashboard
- View productivity trends
- Track completion rates
- Identify patterns and improvements

✓ Full Offline Support
- Works without internet
- Data syncs when connected

✓ Smart Reminders
- Never miss a deadline
- Customizable notification times

All features are completely FREE!

Download Zen Planner today and start achieving your goals!
